dead man’s hat :: corners + nglyg

a double music video i produced for the underground indie band dead man’s hat, directed by the talented elena toncheva + dop vladimir trifonov. it is an attempt at depicting how schizophrenia feels, after months of case studies. i came up with the idea to combine two songs for this video - one from their old album and one from their new ep. the first part of the video is an homage to '50s horror films. first work meeting was in feb 2020, the shoot got postponed 2 times due to covid, shot in nov. 2020.
